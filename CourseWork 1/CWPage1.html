Cast

Tobey Maguire

Tobey Maguire is the lead and titular role as spiderman within the film and brings a youthful enthusiasm to the role that is considered by many to be the most faithful portrayal of the character yet. Tobey, aged 26 at the time, would go on to star in the trillogy of films by director Sam Raimi from 2002 to 2007 before reprising the iconic role.

Tobey, in an interview with the BBC over his commitment to the franchise after the first films success said;

'I don't really feel any pressure. Director Sam Raimi is the perfect choice to protect the spirit of Spider-Man because he's a fan. We did our best to please everybody and not just the die-hard Spider-Man fans, and I think we've done a good job.'


Kirsten Dunst

Kirsten Dunst, who was 19 at the time, plays the Female lead and love interest of Peter, Mary Jane Watson. throughout the film she acts as Spiderman's montivation as well as the person he is trying to hide his identity from in order to protect. Kirsten had previously been a child model and had her acting breakthrough opposite Tom Cruise and Brad Pitt in Interview with the Vampire released in 1994. she later starred in numerous films throughout the late 90's and early 2000's before being cast by Sam Raimi in the film.
