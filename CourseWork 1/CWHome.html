Spider-Man (2002) was one of the most influential superhero films ever to be craeted and if often credited with being the cause of the booming superhero genre today. The film takes place in New York City and follows Photographer and everyday loser, Peter Parker. Peter is your regular highschool senior and struggles with bullying from 'Flash' Thomson until one day his highschool takes a trip to columbia university when he is bitten my a genetically engineered 'super spider'. Following this event, Peter learns that he no longer suffers from short sightedness and has developed seemingly overnight in terms of musculature. the next day in school he discovers a further extent to his new developments, with him being able to shoot webs from his wrists and having increadibly acute reactions. in a later conflict with 'flash' Thomson he realises that he has gained the abilities of a spider. From this event Peter's uncle Ben provides one of the most iconic speeches in film history, 'with Great Power comes Great Responsibility' before tragically dying in a fatal mugging. this devostates Peter and leads him to embrace his new super powers to protect New York City from Crime!
